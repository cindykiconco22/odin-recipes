Chicken Alfredo Pasta


Description

Ingredients
  • fetuccine noodles
  • heavy cream
  • butter
  • black pepper
  • cheese
  • parsley
Steps
  1. In a pan over medium-high heat, melt butter, then add the chicken breast.
  2. Remove from heat and set chicken aside.
  3. Cook until the garlic begins to soften.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil, and stir well to incorporate.
  5. Pour the sauce over cooked penne pasta, add the chicken and mix well.
  6. Add parsley and extra parmesan. Mix well.
  7. Enjoy!
